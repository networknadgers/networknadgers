This is the recipe for some delicious lasagna

Lasagna takes time and effort and this lasagna takes minimal time and effort, yet it comes out delicious


Ingredients

  • Pastrami
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Lettuce
  • Guacamole

Steps

  1. Warm up your tortillas on a griddle
  2. Cook your meat and season it with garlic, salt, and pepper
  3. Once meat is hot, add cheese so that it melts
  4. Lay everything on the table to prep
  5. Add the meat to the tortilla
  6. Add sour cream and guacamole on the side of the tortilla
  7. Add lettuce on top
  8. Fold everything and layer on top of each other

And there you have it! Some delicious lasagna, homemade!
